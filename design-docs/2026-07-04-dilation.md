## 1. Feature 5.1 — Time Dilation

### Dilation studies

Stored in `player.dilation.studies` (ids 1–6; **not** cleared by a study
respec — they reset only on Reality). In frontier:

| id | Cost (TT) | Unlocks | Requirement |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 5000 | Time Dilation | any of TS231–234, EC11 **and** EC12 fully completed (5/5), and all-time TT (`maxTheorem`) ≥ 12900 |
| 2–5 | 1e6/1e7/1e8/1e9 | TD5–TD8 | dilation unlocked / previous TD study |
| 6 | — | Reality | **out of frontier** |

This resolves Phase 4's deferral: `td_is_unlocked(tier)` for tiers 5–8 now
reads dilation studies 2–5.

### The dilated run

- Enter (`startDilatedEternity`): rewarded-if-at-goal Eternity with the
  `switchingDilation` special (forced when below the goal), then
  `dilation.active = true`. Unlocks Achievement 136.
- While active, `dilatedValueOf(x) = 10^(sign(log10 x) · |log10 x|^penalty)`
  (penalty `0.75`, ×1.05 with the `dilationPenalty` upgrade) is applied to:
  the **final AD multiplier** (then `ndMultDT` multiplies *after*, being
  "unaffected by dilation"), the **final ID multiplier**, the **final TD
  multiplier**, and **`Tickspeed.current`** (the interval).
- Exit/completion: *every* Eternity while dilated first calls `rewardTP()` —
  `TP = max(TP, baseTP(thisEternity.maxAM) × tachyonGainMultiplier)` with
  `baseTP = (log10(AM)/400)^1.5`, gated on `canEternity` — then clears
  `dilation.active` (unless entering an EC).

### Dilated Time & Tachyon Galaxies

- `DT/s = TP × 2^dtGain-purchases` (the pre-Reality slice of
  `getDilationGainPerSecond`), added every tick.
- `updateTachyonGalaxies()` per tick: `baseTG = max(baseTG, 1 +
  floor(log_thresholdMult(DT/1000)))` (monotonic), `nextThreshold = 1e3 ×
  thresholdMult^baseTG` where `thresholdMult = 1 + (3.65 · 0.8^galaxyThreshold
  + 0.35)`; `totalTG = min(baseTG × double, 1000) + max(baseTG × double −
  1000, 0)/double` with `double = 2` iff `doubleGalaxies` owned.
- Tachyon Galaxies join `effectiveBaseGalaxies` as free galaxies (tickspeed
  formula + TS41's galaxy count).

### State / save (`player.dilation`)

`studies: Vec<u8>`, `active: bool`, `tachyon_particles/dilated_time/
next_threshold: Decimal`, `base_tachyon_galaxies/total_tachyon_galaxies` (f64
in save; total carries the fractional past-1000 part), `upgrades: Set` (one-
time ids 4–10), `rebuyables: {1,2,3}` (+11–13 Pelle, written back as 0),
`last_ep: Decimal` (display record). Plus `options.confirmations.dilation`.

## 2. Feature 5.2 — Dilation Upgrades

Bought with DT. Rebuyables (geometric costs):

| id | Base | ×/buy | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 dtGain | 1e4 | 10 | ×2^n DT gain |
| 2 galaxyThreshold | 1e6 | 100 | threshold mult factor `0.8^n` (cap 38 → 0); purchase **resets DT and TGs** |
| 3 tachyonGain | 1e7 | 20 | ×3^n TP gain |

One-time:

| id | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 4 doubleGalaxies | 5e6 | ×2 TGs up to 500 base |
| 5 tdMultReplicanti | 1e9 | TD × 10^(0.1·log10(replicantiMult)), softcap 9000 |
| 6 ndMultDT | 5e7 | AD × DT^308 (min 1), after dilation |
| 7 ipMultDT | 2e12 | IP × DT^1000 (min 1) |
| 8 timeStudySplit | 1e10 | all three Dimension-split paths |
| 9 dilationPenalty | 1e11 | dilation exponent 0.75 → 0.7875 (×1.05) |
| 10 ttGenerator | 1e15 | TT += TP/20000 per second |

Pelle upgrades (11–15) are out of frontier.
