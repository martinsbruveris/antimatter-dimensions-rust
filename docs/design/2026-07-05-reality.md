## Frontier decisions

The Reality feature set is deeply entangled with celestials. We port the layer
as it behaves for a player who has realitied but touched no celestial:

- **Glyph types:** the 5 basic types (power / infinity / replication / time /
  dilation) plus the one-off **companion** glyph. Effarig, reality, cursed and
  music glyphs, cosmetics, glyph alteration and alchemy/refining are celestial
  content â€” out.
- **Glyph filter / auto-processor:** Effarig-gated â€” out. Manual glyph choice
  only (plus the deterministic no-START pick).
- **Imaginary Machines + Imaginary Upgrades:** gated on the RM hardcap
  (`1e1000` RM); far past the frontier â€” out (save fields round-trip).
- **BH inversion / auto-pause modes:** inversion needs Imaginary Upgrade 24 /
  Ra; auto-pause is a QoL setting tied to it. We port pause/unpause (with the
  5 s acceleration ramp) and leave inversion/auto-pause out.
- **Perks:** the full 35-node tree is ported (purchasing + tree rules). Perks
  whose effect targets a system we don't have yet (TT autobuyer, EC
  auto-completion, dilation/ID/replicanti autobuyer speedups) get their state
  tracked and their effects marked *deferred* â€” same convention as Break
  Infinity's deferred effects.
- **Reality Upgrades:** all 25 ported (requirements, locks, effects). RU13's
  autobuyer improvements and RU25's Reality autobuyer were initially deferred;
  Automator Stage A (`2026-07-05-automator.md`) implemented the Eternity and
  Reality autobuyers, wiring RU13's mode unlock and RU25. RU13's TD / Ã—5-EP
  autobuyers remain deferred.
- **Speedrun / news / cosmetic extras:** out.

### RNG (`glyph-generator.js` + `math.js`)

