# Phase 7 (Celestial 6): Lai'tela + Imaginary Upgrades

Lai'tela is the sixth Celestial: a parallel **Dark Matter Dimension** economy
(4 tiers producing Dark Matter + Dark Energy in real time), **Singularities**
condensed from Dark Energy that fund a 30-entry **Singularity Milestone** tree,
**Continuum** (fractional continuous dimension purchases), and Lai'tela's Reality
(a destabilization run that disables the top Antimatter/Infinity/Time Dimensions).
Lai'tela unlocks via **Imaginary Upgrade 15**, so this feature also ports the
Imaginary Machines currency + the 25 Imaginary Upgrades (Feature 6.4's deferred
half).

Original: `celestials/laitela/{laitela,dark-matter-dimension,singularity}.js` +
`secret-formula/celestials/singularity-milestones.js` +
`imaginary-upgrades.js` + `secret-formula/reality/imaginary-upgrades.js`, and the
`DarkMatterDimensions.tick` / `laitelaRealityTick` calls in `game.js`.

## 0. Imaginary Machines & Upgrades (`imaginary_upgrades.rs`)

**Imaginary Machines** (`imaginaryMachines`, a Decimal) are gained once RM hits
its cap: `gainedImaginaryMachines` grows with `log10(maxRM)`. In the save the
original encodes iM as `machineRecord = 1e10000 × iM`; we store iM directly and a
separate `maxIM`.

**Upgrades:** ids 1–10 are **rebuyable** (`imaginaryRebuyables[id]`, `cost =
initial · costMult^bought`); ids 11–25 are **one-time** (bit set) gated on a
`checkRequirement` that auto-fires on its event (`GAME_TICK_AFTER` etc.). We port
all 25 costs + purchase + the checkable requirements; effects wired in-frontier:
1–5 (amplifier intensifiers → RU1–5 amplifier bonus), 6 (RM cap ×1e100 per buy),
8 (ID ×1e100000 per buy), 9 (galaxy strength), 10 (singularity gain), 15 (unlock
Lai'tela + Continuum + DMD1), 16–18 (DMD2–4), 19 (annihilation), 20 (iM speed
×10), 21 (annihilation mult from iM), 25 (unlock Pelle — wired in 7.7). The deep
requirement upgrades (11–14, 22–24) store their bit + cost; their requirements
that need unbuilt records are stubbed (never auto-complete). `canLock` re-lock
logic is out of frontier (the bit only sets).

## 1. Dark Matter Dimensions (`laitela.rs`)

4 tiers, each `{ amount, interval_upgrades, power_dm_upgrades, power_de_upgrades,
time_since_last_update, ascension_count }`. DMD`t` unlocks with Imaginary Upgrade
`t+14`. Per real-time tick (`DarkMatterDimensions.tick`, top-down): when
`timeSinceLastUpdate ≥ interval`, `ticks = floor(elapsed/interval)` fire — tier 1
adds `amount·ticks·powerDM` to Dark Matter, tiers 2–4 to the tier below; each adds
`ticks·powerDE` to Dark Energy. `dim4Generation` milestone adds passive DM4.

Formulas (`dark-matter-dimension.js`): `rawInterval = 1000·4^(t−1)·
0.92^intervalUpgrades · ascensionScaling^ascensions · intervalReduction`, floored
at 10 ms; `powerDM`, `powerDE` per the source (ascension ×500/tier, common dark
mult from milestones, reality reward, `darkMatterMult`); costs use the tier's
`adjustedStartingCost` × geometric scaling. Ascension (interval maxed) resets the
interval and multiplies power. Upgrades bought with Dark Matter.

## 2. Singularities & Milestones (`singularity.rs`)

**Condense** (`Singularity.perform`): at `DarkEnergy ≥ cap` (`cap = 200·10^
capIncreases`), reset DE and add `singularitiesGained = floor(gainPerCapIncrease^
capIncreases · singularityMult · (1+iU10))`. `capIncreases` (0–50) is a
player-chosen risk/reward knob.

**Milestones** (30): each `{ start, repeat, increaseThreshold, limit, effect }`.
`completions = min(floor(nerf(rawCompletions)), limit)`, `rawCompletions = 1 +
(ln(sing) − ln(start))/ln(repeat)`, with the `increaseThreshold` soft-nerf
(1/3 rate past the threshold). Unique milestones (`repeat 0`) are one-shot at
`start`. We port the whole catalogue + `completions`/`effect`; the DMD-internal
ones (`darkMatterMult`, `darkEnergyMult`, `darkDimensionCostReduction`,
`darkDimensionIntervalReduction`, `improvedAscensionDM`, `ascensionIntervalScaling`,
`intervalCostScalingReduction`, `realityDEMultiplier`, `multFromInfinitied`,
`darkFrom*`, `dim4Generation`, `improvedSingularityCap`, `singularityMult`) feed
the DMD formulas; the global ones wired in-frontier: `gamespeedFromSingularities`
(game speed), `glyphLevelFromSingularities` (glyph level), `continuumMult`
(matter-extra factor), `theoremPowerFromSingularities`/`infinitiedPow`
(TT/infinity powers). The autobuyer milestones (`darkDimensionAutobuyers`,
`ascensionAutobuyers`, `darkAutobuyerSpeed`, `annihilationAutobuyer`,
`autoCondense`) store completions but their automation is deferred; `darkFrom
Tesseracts`/`tesseractMultFromSingularities`/`boundless` (tesseract targets) are
inert.

## 3. Continuum

Imaginary Upgrade 15 converts Antimatter Dimensions (and Tickspeed) to
**Continuum**: instead of discrete buy-10s, the buy-10 count becomes the
continuous `continuumValue = max(0, 1 + (log10(money/perSet) − log10(baseCost))/
log10(costMult)) × matterExtraPurchaseFactor`. Wired into the AD multiplier
(`buy10Value`) and tickspeed (`bought`). Manual dimension/tickspeed purchases are
disabled while continuum is active. `matterExtraPurchaseFactor = 1 + 0.5·
(log10(maxDM)/50)^0.4·(1+continuumMult)`. The super-exponential cost-scaling
branch of `getContinuumValue` is approximated by the linear branch (documented);
frontier magnitudes stay in the linear region.

## 4. Entropy & Lai'tela's Reality

`entropyGainPerSecond = clamp((log10(AM+1)/1e11)², 0, 100)/200`. Per real tick
(`laitelaRealityTick`), while running, entropy rises; at `entropy ≥ 1` the run is
**destabilized**: record `thisCompletion`, clear the run; if under 30 s,
`difficultyTier++` (so `maxAllowedDimension = 8 − difficultyTier` drops, disabling
one more top dimension in *every* subsequent Lai'tela run). During the run,
Antimatter/Infinity/Time Dimensions with `tier > maxAllowedDimension` produce 0.
`realityReward = max(100^difficultyTier · (360/fastestCompletion)², 1)` — the
`darkMatterMult`-adjacent DMD boost. Entering resets entropy to 0.

**Annihilation** (`annihilate`): with Imaginary Upgrade 19 and DM ≥ 1e60, add
`darkMatterMultGain` to the permanent `darkMatterMult` and reset the DMDs.
`darkMatterMultGain = (log10(DM/1e60)+1)^1.5 · iU21`.

## 5. Save / load

`LaitelaState` (+ `imaginary_upgrades` on the Reality DTO) round-trips
`player.celestials.laitela` (darkMatter/maxDarkMatter, dimensions array, entropy,
difficultyTier, thisCompletion/fastestCompletion, darkMatterMult, darkEnergy,
singularities, singularityCapIncreases, run) and `player.reality.imaginary*`.
`#[serde(default)]` so pre-Lai'tela saves load.

## 6. UI

A **Lai'tela** subtab: the DM/DE header + singularity readout + condense button,
the 4 DMD panels (amount, interval/powerDM/powerDE upgrades, ascend), the
annihilation button, the continuum toggle, difficulty/entropy readout, and the
Singularity Milestone list. An **Imaginary Upgrades** subtab under Reality (the
5×5 grid). Vendored CSS; the milestone auto-sort UI is simplified to a flat list.

## 7. Deferred (frontier cuts) — summary

Continuum super-exponential branch (linear approximation); DMD/ascension/
annihilation/condense autobuyers + their milestones' automation; tesseract-linked
milestones + `boundless` (inert); the deep imaginary-upgrade requirement upgrades
(11–14, 22–24) and `canLock` re-lock; the singularity-milestone auto-notify/sort;
quotes. Imaginary Upgrade 25 (unlock Pelle) lands with 7.7.
