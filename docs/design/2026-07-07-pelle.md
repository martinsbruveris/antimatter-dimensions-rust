# Phase 7 (Celestial 7): Pelle — The Doomed

Pelle is the final Celestial and the game's endgame. **Dooming** a Reality is
permanent: it disables most prior mechanics and replaces prestige with
**Armageddon**, which grants **Remnants**. Remnants generate **Reality Shards**
(Pelle's spendable currency), fund **Pelle Upgrades**, and — via five **Rifts**
that fill from your currencies and cross **Strike** thresholds — build toward the
**Galaxy Generator** and the antimatter **game-end**.

Original: `celestials/pelle/{pelle,rifts,strikes,galaxy-generator,game-end}.js` +
`secret-formula/celestials/{rifts,pelle-upgrades,strikes,galaxy-generator}.js`.

**Unlock.** `Pelle.isUnlocked` = Imaginary Upgrade 25 bought (wired in 7.6).

## 0. Scope & the `isDisabled` sweep

Pelle's defining behavior is that dooming **disables ~30 mechanics** across the
whole engine (`Pelle.isDisabled(mechanic)` guards scattered through dimensions,
prestige, glyphs, studies, celestials, …). Porting every guard is a large,
tedious cross-cutting sweep. **This port models the self-contained Pelle *layer*
faithfully** — dooming/armageddon, Remnants/Reality Shards, the 5 Rifts, Strikes,
Pelle Upgrades, the Galaxy Generator, and the game-end — and exposes
`is_doomed()` + `pelle_is_disabled(mechanic)` as queries. The high-impact seams
are wired (the doomed AD/ID `^0.5` Strike penalties, the rift effects on IP/EP/
replicanti/TD/all-dim, the `antimatterDimensionMult` rebuyable); the full
disable-everything sweep is a **documented cut** (`pelle_is_disabled` returns the
right answer but only a subset of call sites consult it). Cosmetics — the credits
sequence, its song, the `zalgo`/`wordShift` text corruption — are **cut entirely**.

## 1. State (`PelleState`, `player.celestials.pelle`)

`doomed`, `remnants: f64`, `reality_shards: Decimal`, `records:
{ total_antimatter, total_infinity_points, total_eternity_points }` (Decimal),
`upgrades: u32` (one-time bit set), `rebuyables: [u32; 5]` (the 5 Pelle
rebuyables) + `gg_rebuyables: [u32; 5]` (galaxy-generator upgrades),
`rifts: [Rift; 5]` (`{ fill: Decimal, active, reduced_to, percentage_spent }`),
`progress_bits: u32` (Strikes), `galaxy_generator: { unlocked, spent_galaxies,
generated_galaxies, phase, sacrifice_active }`.

## 2. Dooming & Armageddon

`doom_reality()` (from iU25 or the modal): set `doomed`, route through the Reality
reset (`finishProcessReality`), respec studies, reset IP, clear celestial runs. We
model the flag + the reset; the doomed-glyph grant / glyph-space checks / tab
unhiding are cut.

`armageddon(gain)`: if `gain` and `canArmageddon` (`remnantsGain ≥ 1`), add
`remnantsGain` to Remnants, then a forced Reality reset (the "keep on Armageddon"
Pelle Upgrades gate which resources survive). `remnantsGain` from the doomed
records: `((log10(am+2)+log10(ip+2)+log10(ep+2))/1.64)^7.5 − remnants`
(am/ip/ep are `log10` of the doomed totals, ×500/×10/×5 with the dilation Strike).

`realityShardGain(r) = (10^(r^(1/7.5)·4) − 1)/1e3`; Reality Shards accrue at
`realityShardGainPerSecond` while doomed (`gameLoop`).

## 3. Rifts (5)

Each rift fills from a currency (`vacuum`←IP, `decay`←Replicanti, `chaos`←decay%,
`recursion`←EP, `paradox`←DT), draining it 3%/s into `fill` (capped at the fill
for 100%). `percentage(fill)` and `percentageToFill(pct)` per the source; the rift
is only *active* (fillable) once its Strike is unlocked, and at most 2 active at
once. Each rift's `effect(fill)` and 3 `milestones` (unlocked at percentage
thresholds) are ported: vacuum→IP gain + Replicanti uncap + EP gain; decay→
Replicanti speed + ID + RG cap; chaos→TD mult; recursion→EP formula divisor + DB/
ID power + Galaxy-Generator unlock; paradox→all-dim power + TP-from-DT + IP
conversion. Effects wired at their engine sites behind `is_doomed()`.

## 4. Strikes

`progress_bits`: five Strikes unlocked (`trigger`) the first time you reach the
milestone while doomed — Infinity (crunch), power-up Galaxies, Eternity, 115 TT,
Dilate. Each unlocks its rift and applies a penalty (AD/ID `^0.5`, replicanti
slowdown, EC IP nerf, permanent dilation). We trigger them from the crunch/
eternity/dilation paths behind `is_doomed()` and wire the AD/ID `^0.5` penalties.

## 5. Pelle Upgrades

5 rebuyable (`antimatterDimensionMult` cap 44, `timeSpeedMult` 35, `glyphLevels`
26, `infConversion` 21, `galaxyPower` 9) with the `expWithIncreasedScale` cost,
and 23 one-time (mostly "keep X on Armageddon" + autobuyer unlocks). Bought with
Reality Shards. The rebuyable effects wired; the "keep on Armageddon" gates are
consulted by the Armageddon reset (a subset in-frontier).

## 6. Galaxy Generator

Unlocked by the recursion rift's 3rd milestone. Generates galaxies at
`gainPerSecond` (additive × multiplicative × antimatter/IP/EP-Mult upgrades),
capped per `phase` by the rifts' `galaxyGeneratorThreshold`s (paradox 1e5 <
vacuum 1000 < … sorted). `sacrifice` drains the capping rift to advance the phase.
`galaxies = generated − spent`. Its 5 upgrades are bought with generated galaxies
/ AM / IP / EP.

## 7. Game-end

`GameEnd.endState = max((log10(log10(doomedTotalAM+1)+1) − 8.7)/(log10(9e15) −
8.7), 0)`; at `endState ≥ 1` the game ends (`is_game_end`). We compute `endState`
and set the flag; the credits/song/animation are cut.

## 8. Save / load

`PelleDTO` round-trips `player.celestials.pelle` (doomed, upgrades set, remnants,
realityShards, records, rebuyables, rifts, progressBits, galaxyGenerator).
`is_game_end` ↔ `player.isGameEnd`. `#[serde(default)]` so pre-Pelle saves load.

## 9. UI

A **Pelle** subtab: the doom button (pre-doom) → the Remnants / Reality Shards
header, the Armageddon button, the 5 rift bars (fill %, toggle, milestones), the
Pelle Upgrade grid, and (once unlocked) the Galaxy Generator. The zalgo/credits
end sequence is cut.

## 10. Deferred (frontier cuts) — summary

The full `isDisabled` disable-everything sweep (subset wired); the doomed-glyph
grant + glyph-space/harshAutoClean logic; the credits/song/zalgo/wordShift
cosmetics; the special Pelle glyph effect; several deep rift-milestone effects
whose targets are unbuilt; the Galaxy-Generator glyph-slot refresh nicety.
